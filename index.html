Our Aims

Comprehensive plans to improve all aspects of school life

Academic

  • Create a platform for 5th & 3rd formers to get advice from upperclassmen about subject selection
  • Provide more university information and exam tips (UCAT, IMAT) from students who took them

Leisure

  • Implement Casual Fridays for a more relaxing end to the week
  • Move Diapason to Winter so more students can attend
  • Organize monthly themed events (Car Day, Sports Day, Music Day)
  • Hold Greek Day twice to celebrate both national holidays properly

Sports

  • Organize more tournaments (volleyball, basketball, padel) to include everyone
  • Create friendly competitions against other schools

General

  • Add variety of new snacks to cafeteria including healthier options
  • Improve toilet facilities and provide feminine hygiene products

Student Support

Building bridges between students for better guidance and support

Student2Student

We've created this Instagram platform so younger students can ask questions about exams or subject selection, building helpful relationships between students. As 7th form students who faced difficulties without enough guidance, we want to share our experiences and show we're here to support you.
